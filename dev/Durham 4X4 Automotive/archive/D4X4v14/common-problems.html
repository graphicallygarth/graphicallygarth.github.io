How to use these guides

Each section below outlines the top issues we see for that model, what typically causes them, symptoms to look for, and how we fix them. When a smart upgrade prevents repeat problems, we’ll point it out.

CJ — Classic and Capable

Time-tested rigs that benefit from careful inspections and smart upgrades.

Carb tuning and hot starts

Symptoms: Hard hot starts, rough idle, fuel smell.

Cause: Heat soak and worn carb components.

Fix: Proper tune, fuel line routing, heat shielding, or EFI conversion.

Steering wander

Symptoms: Loose steering, constant correction on highway.

Cause: Worn tie rods, steering box play, alignment.

Fix: End-to-end steering refresh, correct caster, quality parts.

CJ Gallery

YJ — Square headlights, solid basics

Simple, durable platforms that respond well to preventative maintenance.

Leaf spring sag and ride harshness

Symptoms: Sagging stance, bouncy ride, uneven tire wear.

Cause: Aged leaf packs and shocks.

Fix: New springs/shocks matched to use, proper shackle angle.

4.0L idle and sensor issues

Symptoms: Surging idle, stalling when warm.

Cause: Dirty IAC, failing TPS, vacuum leaks.

Fix: Smoke test, replace sensors, throttle body service.

YJ Gallery

TJ — Trail-ready with modern comforts

A favorite for weekend wheeling and daily use.

Death wobble

Symptoms: Violent front-end shake after hitting a bump.

Cause: Loose track bar, worn joints, out-of-spec caster.

Fix: Systematic front-end inspection, track bar/bushing replacement, alignment.

Oil leaks (rear main, valve cover)

Symptoms: Oil spots under Jeep, burning oil smell.

Cause: Aged seals and gaskets on high-mile 4.0L engines.

Fix: Proper seal replacement, crankcase ventilation check.

TJ Gallery

LJ — Long-wheelbase TJ

Great platform for overlanding with a few known quirks.

Driveline vibes after lift

Symptoms: Vibration at 35–60 mph post-lift.

Cause: Pinion/shaft angles, stock driveshaft limitations.

Fix: SYE + double-cardan shaft, correct pinion angle, motor mounts.

Brake fade on long descents

Symptoms: Soft pedal, longer stopping distances when loaded.

Cause: Heat buildup and stock components at their limit.

Fix: Performance pads/rotors, quality fluid, caliper service.

LJ Gallery

JK — Versatile and upgradable

Handles family duty and weekend trails with the right setup.

Exhaust manifold cracks

Symptoms: Ticking on cold start, exhaust smell.

Cause: Thermal cycling and factory welds.

Fix: Upgraded manifold, new hardware, heat shielding as needed.

Cooling system inefficiency

Symptoms: Running hot in traffic or towing.

Cause: Aging radiator/fans, debris in fins.

Fix: Radiator/fan service or replacement, coolant flush, airflow check.

JK Gallery

JL — Modern tech, still a Jeep

Excellent platform that benefits from early maintenance and careful upgrades.

Electrical gremlins after accessories

Symptoms: Intermittent lights, warning messages, battery drain.

Cause: Add-on wiring without proper integration.

Fix: Clean wiring, correct fusing/relays, battery/ground upgrades.

Turbo 2.0T heat management

Symptoms: Heat soak, reduced performance on hot days.

Cause: Intake temps and factory heat shielding limits.

Fix: Intercooler checks, heat shielding, service intervals, software updates.
